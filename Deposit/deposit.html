How to Deposit Crypto

Follow these steps to safely deposit crypto into your wallet:

  1. Access Your Wallet: Open your crypto wallet and navigate to the "Deposit" section.
  2. Obtain Your Address: Copy your wallet's deposit address or scan the provided QR code. Always verify the address before proceeding.
  3. Initiate the Transfer: From your crypto exchange or another wallet, send the desired amount of crypto to the copied address.
  4. Confirm the Transaction: Ensure you review the transaction details and confirm. Wait for the required network confirmations.
  5. Monitor Your Balance: Once confirmed, your deposited funds will reflect in your wallet balance. This may take a few minutes depending on network traffic.

Please note: Always double-check your wallet address and transaction details. If you encounter any issues, contact our support team for assistance.
